# Privacy Policy for Claude.ai RTL Support

**Last Updated**: November 1, 2024

## Overview

Claude.ai RTL Support ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The Extension:
- ✅ Does NOT collect any personal information
- ✅ Does NOT track your browsing activity
- ✅ Does NOT use analytics or telemetry
- ✅ Does NOT communicate with external servers
- ✅ Does NOT share data with third parties

## Data Storage

All extension settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API:
- RTL preferences (chat input, main content, side panel)
- Panel position settings
- Per-chat RTL configurations

**All data stays on your device and never leaves your browser.**

## Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions:

### Storage Permission
- **Purpose**: Save your RTL preferences locally
- **Scope**: Local browser storage only
- **Data**: RTL toggle states and panel position

### Host Permission (claude.ai)
- **Purpose**: Apply RTL styling to Claude.ai pages
- **Scope**: Only on claude.ai domain
- **Data**: No data is collected or transmitted

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.
